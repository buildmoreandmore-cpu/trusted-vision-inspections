About Us

The Person Behind Every Inspection

When you hire Trusted Vision, you're not getting a faceless company. You're getting Carl Hall -- hands-on, thorough, and personally invested in protecting your home purchase.

ASHI Certified Inspector

2,500+ inspections completed

Carl Hall

Owner & Lead Inspector

I started Trusted Vision Home Inspections because I believe every homebuyer deserves to know exactly what they're getting before they sign. Too many people close on a home only to discover costly surprises weeks later. That's not right -- and it's what I'm here to prevent.

With over 2,500 inspections under my belt and more than 15 years in the industry, I've seen every issue a home can hide. From cracked foundations in older Decatur bungalows to improper wiring in new-build McMansions north of the city, I've caught the problems that other inspectors miss.

Every inspection I do follows a detailed 400+ point checklist. I personally walk every property, check every system, and document everything with photos so you have a clear picture of what you're buying. And I don't just hand you a report and disappear -- I walk you through my findings, explain what matters, what doesn't, and what you should negotiate on.

My commitment is simple: you'll never wonder "what if" after your inspection. You'll know.
